Leana Santana// Digital Design and Animation Student

Background

Leana Santana is a student at Hostos Community College, located in the south Bronx. She moved to the Bronx in the summer of 2018 to pursue higher education. At Hostos, she majors in Digital Design and Animation, taking classes in relation to traditional and digital art. She has two more semesters at this school before transferring to an undergraduate college.

Achievements

Getting involved has been one of Leana's major goals while attending college, and within the small time of two semesters she has achieved alot. During the Fall 2018 semester, Leana volunteered as much as she could when her schedule allowed it. Not only that, During the Spring 2019 semester she was elected Executive Secretary of the Student Government Association, inaugrated into the Phi Theta Kappa Honor society, and welcomed into the Cuny Service Corp.

Goals

Leana Santana's Goals is to one day become a VFX artist for feature film. However, she is currently looking for any opportunity possible to bring her creative ideas to life. She is also passionate about public policy, and one day hopes to have art and politics collide into a career.
